About us

QUANTA is a part of departmental association, the main motto of Quanta is to develop a hands on technical knowledge that encourages a person to move on with logical aptitude and to have a good command over Robotics, Embedded system

It is of utmost importance to realize that boundaries are mere perceptions. The shattering of constraints and barriers imposed on oneself is the first step to realizing the boundless infinity of the fascinating world of Electronics & Communication. Creative thought and vision sow the seed of progress - but it is opportunity and constant endeavour which nourishes development.

On this site you'll find information about our coming activities and editorials on exciting topics related to field of electronics, along with descriptions of our special lecture series. We hope you will find all of the information you are looking for .
